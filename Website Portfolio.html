    1st family photo

    3rd family photo

    Eclipse Disaster

    In this image I created a more fantasy aspect with the idea for the image being what the title says, an "eclipse disaster". I took an image of a view of New York City,
    then I added a flood image and added a layer mask to only show the water. I used an image of a cloudy sky and put it over the original sky with the use of a layer mask.
    Then I used the "brightness/conrast" adjusment tool, then I finally added a picture of the eclipse to finish the image.
